Lightning Labs

...it is the Soldiers in the field - those who have to fight - who should play a major role in determining what equipment they need to have and how they will operate.

-Jon T. Hoffman, A History of Innovation


The National Defense Strategy (NDS) makes clear that the increasingly complex and disordered battlefield of the near-future requires a more lethal, resilient, and rapidly innovating force. The 25th Infantry Division has therefore began an innovation program, and Lightning Labs is one part of that. Lightning Labs builds on the successes and failures of existing programs of other units.

Lightning Labs believes that great ideas can come from anyone, anywhere, irrespective of rank or position. Soldiers possess the tactical knowledge to identify opportunities to improve their mission sets, and they should be encouraged and provided the resources to do so.

Lightning Labs is looking for talented Soldiers, specifically Soldiers with software development experience! Please contact us through the email below if you believe you will be a good fit for Lightning Labs.
