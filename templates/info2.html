Tropic Lightning Week 2023

Honoring Our Legacy. Relentless Pursuit of Excellence.

28 November - 1 December 2023
Schofield Barracks, Hawaii

The 25th Infantry Division was formed on 1 October 1941 at Schofield Barracks from its antecedent, the Hawaiian Division. One year following the 7 December 1941 Japanese attack on Oahu, the Division went into action at Guadalcanal where it earned the nickname ‘Tropic Lightning’ for its quick defeat of the enemy. America’s Pacific Division will celebrate its 82nd birthday with a four-day series of events called ‘Tropic Lightning Week’.

This four-day event will result in one Battalion being named the Guadalcanal Cup Champion.
